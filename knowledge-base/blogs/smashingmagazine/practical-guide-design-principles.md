# A Practical Guide To Design Principles

**Date:** April 1, 2026
**Author:** Vitaly Friedman
**Source:** https://www.smashingmagazine.com/2026/04/practical-guide-design-principles/

## 8-Step Workshop for Establishing Principles

1. Pre-session Research — Study user language
2. Get Into Principles Mode — Small group brainstorming
3. Product Analogies — Compare products to tangible items
4. Extract Attributes — Silent writing of initial principles
5. Link to Research — Ground in actual user needs
6. Value Statements — "We want X because of Y"
7. Move to Principles — Refine into enduring rules
8. Reality Check — Validate against existing products

Resource: Principles.design — 230 pointers for design principles.
