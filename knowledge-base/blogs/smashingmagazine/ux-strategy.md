# Six Key Components of UX Strategy

**Date:** November 5, 2025
**Author:** Vitaly Friedman
**Source:** https://www.smashingmagazine.com/2025/11/practical-guide-ux-strategy/

## Key Distinctions
- **Goals** = desired outcomes
- **Strategy** = high-level solutions
- **Plans** = implementation steps

## Six Essential Components
1. Target goal — improved future UX state
2. User segments — primary audience focus
3. Priorities — what to do AND what to avoid
4. High-value actions — solutions for user and business needs
5. Feasibility — realistic resource assessment
6. Risks — identifying potential bottlenecks

UX strategy frames design as a business differentiator, speaking the language of metrics and objectives.
