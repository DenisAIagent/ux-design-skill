# Top UX Design Voices Worth Following in 2026

**Date:** June 25, 2025
**Author:** Ebony Boadu
**Source:** https://www.lyssna.com/blog/ux-design-influencers/

---

## Article Overview

This comprehensive guide highlights 16 influential UX design professionals whose content offers valuable insights for designers at all career stages. The featured creators cover diverse specialties including design systems, research methodologies, career development, and practical UI/UX techniques across multiple platforms.

## Featured Designers

### 1. Adham Dannaway
**Platform:** LinkedIn and X

Adham bridges design and development expertise, focusing on "design systems and recommending the right tools and programs to build scalable, consistent UI components." His thoughtful UI critiques encourage fresh perspectives on common design patterns.

### 2. Emilie Mazurek
**Platform:** LinkedIn

Known for candid storytelling about UX professional life, Emilie combines strategy with hands-on research tips, addressing real obstacles like "stakeholder buy-in or research hurdles" that designers encounter daily.

### 3. Aneta Kmiecik
**Platforms:** LinkedIn and Instagram

Aneta delivers visual, process-driven content through bite-sized Instagram carousels and deeper LinkedIn discussions about design feedback, collaboration, and workflow efficiency—all presented with humor.

### 4. Arash Ahadzadeh
**Platform:** YouTube (DesignWithArash)

With over 10 years experience, Arash offers comprehensive tutorials on "foundational design principles, Figma mastery, interactive prototyping, and animations" alongside free resources.

### 5. Abraham John
**Platform:** LinkedIn

Abraham curates design resources, UI libraries, and emerging platforms, sharing "bite-sized takes on usability, interaction, and product thinking" to spark inspiration.

### 6. Florian Bolter
**Platform:** LinkedIn

Balancing practical UX advice with career guidance, Florian addresses topics like user flows, design communication, and career challenges for early-to-mid-career professionals.

### 7. Femke van Schoonhoven
**Platforms:** Instagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn

Femke shares real-world product team lessons, specializing in "stakeholder communication" and "user research methodologies that actually get results."

### 8. Samaneh Dehghanpour
**Platform:** LinkedIn

Focused on empowering solo and in-house designers, Samaneh provides tips on Figma workflows, design confidence, and independent project management.

### 9. Christopher Nguyen
**Platform:** LinkedIn

Christopher connects UX with "business strategy and user psychology," exploring how design influences decisions and drives business outcomes.

### 10. Aida Abazi
**Platform:** Instagram

Aida creates highly visual, accessible content targeting junior designers through "relatable design scenarios" presented in friendly, easy-to-digest formats.

### 11. Joelle Phua
**Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, and Discord

Joelle delivers "straight-talking, self-taught UX advice" covering portfolios, research, developer handoff, and job hunting with templates and practical guides.

### 12. Jesse Showalter
**Platforms:** YouTube and Instagram

Known for high-energy tutorials, Jesse focuses on "Figma, Webflow, and front-end UI/UX practices" with motivational, community-oriented teaching.

### 13. Gemma Helyer
**Platforms:** YouTube, LinkedIn, and Instagram

Gemma champions "accessible and inclusive design" through tutorials, case studies, and career reflections emphasizing ethical, user-centered products.

### 14. Juxtopposed
**Platform:** YouTube

Specializing in app redesigns, Juxtopposed offers "thoughtful design critiques and visual storytelling" that inspire improved visual thinking and user-first approaches.

### 15. Nolan Perkins
**Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn

Creating fast, digestible content for junior designers, Nolan addresses industry myths and portfolio advice in energetic, highly watchable formats.

### 16. Jan Mraz
**Platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and LinkedIn

Jan's clean, tactical carousels break down "design tools, UX workflows, and real-world case studies" in structured, practical ways.
