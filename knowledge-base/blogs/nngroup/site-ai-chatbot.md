# What Is Your Site's AI Chatbot for? Users Can't Tell

**Date:** March 20, 2026
**Authors:** Maria Rosala, Georgia Kenderova, Tanner Kohler
**Source:** https://www.nngroup.com/articles/site-ai-chatbot/

## Key Findings

- **Discoverability Issues:** Small icons, missing labels, inconsistent placement
- **Skepticism:** "Most people have been exposed to the bad version"
- **Unclear Value:** Vague messages leave users uncertain
- **Task Misalignment:** Offering help users don't seek
- **Efficiency Problems:** Search and filters are often faster

## Where Chatbots Succeed

- Product-specific questions on detail pages
- Complex, context-dependent questions
- Unexpected insights users wouldn't find independently

## Recommendations

- Research actual user problems before implementing
- Avoid replicating existing search/filter functionality
- Surface chatbots contextually where they solve specific problems
- Use clear, specific prompts rather than generic openings
