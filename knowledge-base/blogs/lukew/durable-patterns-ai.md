# Durable Patterns in AI Product Design

**Date:** March 12, 2026
**Author:** Luke Wroblewski
**Source:** https://lukew.com/ff/entry.asp?2145

Major technology platform shifts fundamentally transform what constitutes an "application." AI represents another such shift, evolving at unprecedented speed.

## Evolution Stages

### Stage 1: Behind-the-Scenes AI
Early AI improvements operated invisibly. Google Translate's 2016 overhaul, YouTube recommendations — ML remained backstage infrastructure.

### Stage 2: Direct Conversational Interaction
ChatGPT established chat as the dominant AI pattern. Durable patterns emerged:

- **Suggested Questions:** Help users understand system capabilities
- **Citation Integration:** Showing sources significantly boosts trust
- **Multimedia Responses:** Images, diagrams, interactive elements improve comprehension

Three persistent core challenges: capability awareness, context awareness, and information overload.

### Stage 3: Agentic Systems
Tool-enabled AI dramatically increases complexity. User responses split: those wanting visibility into system thinking vs. those preferring just results.

Design solutions evolved:
- Collapsed tool calls with optional expansion
- Separated reasoning (left panel) from results (right panel)

## Capability Awareness Solutions
- **Prompt Enhancement:** Systems rewrite basic input into detailed instructions
- **Visual Task Builders:** Compose compound instructions from capability columns
- **Intelligent Onboarding:** AI handles empty-state experiences

## Context Awareness
Spatial attention specification — users highlight objects, drag references, direct models toward specific areas.

## Agent Orchestration
Coordinator agents plan work, launch sub-agents executing tasks simultaneously. Open questions about human cognitive limits managing multiple CEO-level agents.
