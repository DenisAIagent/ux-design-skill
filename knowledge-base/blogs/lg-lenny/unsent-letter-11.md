# Unsent Letter #11

*Date: 2025-07-02*

*To the maestro who passed me by*

---

When I first met you, I tried to stand taller. Look bigger.

You were quiet as a breeze, your manner good-natured, your visage a buddha smile below crinkled eyes. But something about you felt larger than life
