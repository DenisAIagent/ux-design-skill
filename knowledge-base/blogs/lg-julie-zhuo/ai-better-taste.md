# When AI Has Better Taste Than You

**Date:** June 24, 2025
**Author:** Julie Zhuo
**Source:** https://lg.substack.com/p/when-ai-has-better-taste-than-you

## The Three Elements Framework

1. **Capabilities** — Skills and knowledge we possess
2. **Taste** — Our values, preferences, and aesthetic judgments
3. **Agency** — Our will and motivation to act

## AI's Dominance in Capabilities

AI now produces writing, artwork, and code that rivals human expertise. The trajectory suggests continued expansion.

## The Fragility of Taste

"Great taste operates through pattern recognition across vast cultural knowledge," making it vulnerable to replication. However, tastemakers who champion initially unpopular positions remain unique.

## Agency as Humanity's Remaining Moat

Genuine human agency — our capacity to determine purpose and act according to deeply held values — may be our most irreplaceable advantage. While AI optimizes toward programmed objectives, humans alone generate intentional choice grounded in subjective meaning.
