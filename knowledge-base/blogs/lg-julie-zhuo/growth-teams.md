# What Excellent Growth Teams See That Others Miss

**Date:** March 11, 2026
**Author:** Julie Zhuo
**Source:** https://lg.substack.com/p/what-excellent-growth-teams-see-that

Interview with Brian Hale, Chief Growth Officer at DoorDash — 7 contrasts between mediocre and exceptional growth teams.

## Key Distinctions

**1. Journey vs. Funnel Optimization** — Superior teams obsess about every single step, not just acquiring customers.

**2. Blockage Removal vs. Feature Shipping** — Diagnose customer hesitation rather than making arbitrary changes.

**3. Learning Velocity vs. Experiment Volume** — Quality insights matter more than motion.

**4. Product Growth vs. Growth Hacking** — Remove friction and clarify value through substantive product work.

**5. Relationship Metrics vs. Activity Metrics** — Anchor on MAU to ensure continuous relationship building.

**6. Compounding Wins vs. Celebrating and Moving On** — Analyze surprising successes to identify hidden bottlenecks.

**7. Rigorous Hiring Scrutiny** — Interrogate whether companies possess necessary conditions.

"Excellent growth teams relentlessly do the most important thing, even when it's unglamorous."
