# You're definitely going to be a manager now

**Date:** September 9, 2025
**Author:** Julie Zhuo
**Source:** https://lg.substack.com/p/managing-ai-is-like-managing-humans

## Pure Middle Manager Roles Will Decline

Teams become leaner through AI. Information flows more directly, reducing the "telephone game" effect.

## Management Skills Become Even More Critical

The practice of management — "getting better outcomes from a group" — becomes increasingly vital. ICs must adopt managerial thinking around prioritization, task allocation, and accountability.

"AI rewards high-agency people with a clear sense of purpose."

## Managing AI Resembles Managing Humans

Define clear success criteria, match the right tool to each task, verify quality. Crucial distinction: AI requires no emotional support, working tirelessly without fatigue.

## Leadership Grows Both Easier and Harder

AI provides tireless coaching, but leaders face unprecedented challenges. Must balance "sturdiness and flexibility," remaining calm without minimizing concerns. Human-centered management remains fundamentally unchanged.
