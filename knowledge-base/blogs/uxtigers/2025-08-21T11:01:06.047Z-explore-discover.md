# A New AI: Creation as Exploration and Discovery

**Date:** 2025-08-21T11:01:06.047Z
**Author:** Jakob Nielsen
**Source:** https://www.uxtigers.com/post/explore-discover

---

AI transforms UX into exploration-based discovery. Users will navigate latent solution spaces rather than specifying outcomes. Creation shifts from building or describing to discovering possibilities. Future interfaces become collaborative playgrounds where users recognize solutions instead of articulating visions, augmenting human existence.
