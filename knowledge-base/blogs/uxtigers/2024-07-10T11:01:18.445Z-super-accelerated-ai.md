# The Decade of AI Super-Acceleration

**Date:** 2024-07-10T11:01:18.445Z
**Author:** Jakob Nielsen
**Source:** https://www.uxtigers.com/post/super-accelerated-ai

---

AI's rapid advancement is caused by three factors: raw compute increases, algorithmic efficiency improvements, and "unhobbling" processes. In the current super-accelerated decade, we enjoy a 10,000x AI performance boost every four years, making it likely that AI will exceed top human experts by 2027. There are good arguments that AI progress past 2027 could be slower, but also that it could be even faster.
