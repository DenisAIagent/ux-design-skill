# Design Leaders Should Go “Founder Mode”

**Date:** 2024-11-21T12:01:07.996Z
**Author:** Jakob Nielsen
**Source:** https://www.uxtigers.com/post/founder-mode

---

The future of design leadership is not to aspire to ever-higher executive roles, ruling over a vast UX department. Rather, embrace the “Founder Mode” concept of hands-on and vision-based leadership of small and highly effective design teams.
