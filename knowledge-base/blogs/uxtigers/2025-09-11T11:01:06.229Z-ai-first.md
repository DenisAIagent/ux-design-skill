# AI-First Companies

**Date:** 2025-09-11T11:01:06.229Z
**Author:** Jakob Nielsen
**Source:** https://www.uxtigers.com/post/ai-first

---

AI-First means massive automation to repetitive tasks, rapid iteration over perfection, ubiquitous AI integration across workflows, and workforce transformation requiring AI literacy. As organizations automate routine work, humans shift to oversight roles, creating new UX challenges around maintaining vigilance over increasingly autonomous systems.
