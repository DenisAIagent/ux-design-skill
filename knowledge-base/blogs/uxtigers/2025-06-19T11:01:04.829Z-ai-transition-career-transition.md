# Use the AI Transition Period to Transition Your Career

**Date:** 2025-06-19T11:01:04.829Z
**Author:** Jakob Nielsen
**Source:** https://www.uxtigers.com/post/ai-transition-career-transition

---

In the great UX pivot, you have 5 years to trade yesterday’s expertise for tomorrow’s relevance. Legacy UX skills won’t save you in the AI age, but cultivating agency, judgment, and persuasion will. Most importantly, now is the time to prepare for working with superintelligence, before it’s too late and you become obsolete. Don’t cling to a vanishing past.
