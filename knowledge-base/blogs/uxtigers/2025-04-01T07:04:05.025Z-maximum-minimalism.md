# Maximum Minimalism: The Ultimate Evolution of Intuitive Design

**Date:** 2025-04-01T07:04:05.025Z
**Author:** Jakob Nielsen
**Source:** https://www.uxtigers.com/post/maximum-minimalism

---

Maximum Minimalism is a cutting-edge UX design trend that removes text and images, leaving only buttons for interaction. This streamlined approach leverages spatial memory and instinct to reduce cognitive load, boost intuition, and enhance accessibility, offering a bold evolution of minimalist design principles.
