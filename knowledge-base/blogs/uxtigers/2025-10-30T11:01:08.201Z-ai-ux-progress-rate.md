**Date:** 2025-10-30T11:01:08.201Z
**Author:** Jakob Nielsen

When can AI replace UX designers? We need longitudinal research to compare AI with human experts across multiple UX methods and track the improvement rates. Results could define an AI scaling law for UX skills, helping companies decide when to delegate what UX work to AI versus humans.
