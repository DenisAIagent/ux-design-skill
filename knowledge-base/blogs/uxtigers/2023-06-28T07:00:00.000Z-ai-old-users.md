# Generative AI Enhances Old Users’ Intellectual Performance Through Wise Winnowing

**Date:** 2023-06-28T07:00:00.000Z
**Author:** Jakob Nielsen
**Source:** https://www.uxtigers.com/post/ai-old-users

---

Juice the creative output of old knowledge workers with a wide range of AI-created ideas. Then leverage their superior crystallized intelligence and human judgment for wise winnowing, narrowing these options to select the best solution. Finally, remix with human insight for true human-computer symbiant power. Fluid intelligence peaks around the age of 20, only to decline due to neurological attrition each year after that. Yet 20-year-olds are not the most productive knowledge workers.
