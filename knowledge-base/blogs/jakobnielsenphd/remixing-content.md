# Hamlet Remixed: Using AI to Convert Content into Alternative Formats

**Date:** April 2, 2026
**Source:** https://jakobnielsenphd.substack.com/p/remixing-content

Jakob Nielsen demonstrates how AI enables rapid transformation of a single source material across multiple media formats, styles, and audience frames. Using Shakespeare's Hamlet as a case study.

## Key Transformations

**Medium Changes:** Conversions including comic books, music videos, data visualizations, and posters.

**Style Variations:** Viking-era comic strip and Skaldic poetry, honoring the play's historical roots.

**Audience Reframing:** Tourist posters, tabloid covers, law review articles, trade magazines, and consulting slide decks.

## Production Insights

Nielsen spent approximately $160 generating a four-minute AI music video. "Money often buys latency reduction rather than better craftsmanship" in AI workflows, allowing creators to run parallel generations simultaneously.

## Content Strategy Evolution

Moving beyond "COPE" (Create Once, Publish Everywhere) toward "CORE" (Create Once, Remix Everywhere) — each medium reveals different aspects of source material while demanding rigorous editorial judgment and quality control.
