# The Year in Image Generation

**Date:** 2025-12-29

**Source:** [Original Article](https://jakobnielsenphd.substack.com/p/2025-images)

---

## Summary

Jakob Nielsen presents his twelve best AI-generated images from 2025, one per month, showcasing the progression of image generation technology. He generated approximately 20,000 images across various AI models, with only about 1,500 published--a 7.5% success rate.

## Three Distinct Periods

1. **January-March:** Legacy models like Midjourney could render single subjects beautifully but struggled with multiple concepts in one frame
2. **April-October:** OpenAI's GPT Image-1 dominated, enabling text rendering and scene composition
3. **November-December:** Nano Banana Pro enabled complex layouts, infographics, and sophisticated visual communication

## Winning Models

The top 12 images were created using:
- GPT Image-1: 5 images
- Seedream 4: 3 images
- Midjourney: 2 images
- Leonardo: 1 image
- Nano Banana Pro: 1 image

## Major Shift

Nielsen notes that "image rendering is no longer the hard part. Editorial selection is." With dozens of variations generatable in minutes, the critical resource has become attention and clarity. The year marked a transition "from an era of visual decoration to an era of visual specification."

## Emerging Challenge

While quality improved dramatically, models now produce "hallucinated drawings"--infographics with bars that don't match numbers or process diagrams with nonsensical arrows. This represents a new "Uncanny Valley of Information."
