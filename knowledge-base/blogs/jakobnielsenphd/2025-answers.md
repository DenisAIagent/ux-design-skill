## Overview

Jakob Nielsen presents the 70 correct answers to a quiz about major UX and AI developments in 2025. All questions are based on his published articles, though they can be answered using general knowledge.

## Key Topics Covered

### AI and User Experience Integration

The answers span critical areas where AI reshapes design thinking. "Slow AI" tasks requiring hours or days of processing demand specific design patterns like resumption summaries that help users "reconstruct their mental model" after context switching. A/B testing becomes unreliable when AI output variability increases statistical noise beyond signal detection thresholds.

### Human-AI Collaboration Dynamics

Research reveals a "Trough of Mediocrity" where adding humans to analytical tasks actually degrades performance. Conversely, creative work benefits tremendously from human-AI pairing, where humans provide direction and AI generates volume.

### Economic and Workforce Implications

The "Genii Shift" predicts that routine knowledge workers will face displacement. The "Measurement Gap" highlights how traditional GDP metrics fail to capture welfare gains from zero-cost AI services.

### Practical UX Design Patterns

- **Generative UI** strips away navigation friction by synthesizing interfaces containing "only the elements required to satisfy the user's specific intent"
- **Prompt Augmentation** patterns like Prompt Builders reduce articulation barriers through GUI components rather than pure text
- **Hamburger menus** on desktop violate visibility heuristics by gratuitously hiding available options

### Career Evolution

Design leadership must shift to "Founder Mode," redefining Product-Market Fit internally where "the product is the design organization's output, and the market is the rest of the company."

### Emerging Research Areas

Answers cover novel concepts like "AI Stigma" (identical AI-labeled content rated less reliable than human-labeled), "Shadow AI" (employees secretly using AI tools), and "Calibrated Trust" (appropriate skepticism balanced with AI adoption).
