# UX Roundup: Old vs New UX | Adam Smith Predicts AI | AI Video | AI Agents

**Date:** 2026-03-16

**Source:** [Original Article](https://jakobnielsenphd.substack.com/p/ux-roundup-20260316)

---

## Topics

1. **Old UX Dominates in New Books** - Most recent UX books focus on legacy design practices rather than AI-driven approaches. Nielsen argues the balance is "dramatically skewed toward the old world of UX."

2. **Adam Smith and AI Economics** - Parallels between the Industrial Revolution and AI developments. Smith's theories predict AI will reshape labor, create cognitive division of work, and drive wealth creation while displacing mid-level knowledge workers.

3. **Chinese Sci-Fi Video with Seedance 2** - Impressive science fiction shorts demonstrating visual quality comparable to major studio productions.

4. **Five Levels of Agentic Commerce** - Stripe's framework outlines progression from basic automation through anticipatory purchasing, with current systems at levels 1-2.
