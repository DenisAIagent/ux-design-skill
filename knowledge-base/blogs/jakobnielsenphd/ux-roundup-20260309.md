## Topics

1. **GUI Monument Visualization** - Using Nano Banana 2 to render historical figures (Engelbart, Tesler, Jobs, Andreessen) as a granite monument tribute to GUI pioneers.

2. **AI Video Quality** - Impressive short-form AI videos with improved production quality, though story writing lags behind visuals.

3. **Software Industry Survival** - Despite cheaper AI coding tools, software companies endure because demand expands when costs drop and strong market moats exist.

4. **Bing's GEO Tool** - Microsoft released an "AI Performance" dashboard showing how often websites appear in Copilot answers.

5. **AI Task Horizons** - METR data shows AI capability improvements doubling task completion times to 12 hours, suggesting hybrid human-AI workflows remain valuable.

6. **Google's AI Simplification** - Google streamlined overlapping image generation services, acknowledging prior usability confusion.

7. **NotebookLM Videos** - One-shot AI video generation produces adequate instructional content in seconds versus hours of manual work.

8. **GPT 5.4 Writing** - Latest model demonstrates improved creative writing quality.
