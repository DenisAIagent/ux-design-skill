# 2025 Year in Review: Themes, Trends, Status, Top 10 Articles

**Date:** 2025-12-18

**Source:** [Original Article](https://jakobnielsenphd.substack.com/p/2025-review)

## Overview

Jakob Nielsen published approximately 330,000 words across 100 articles in 2025, exploring how artificial intelligence fundamentally transformed user experience practice. He also produced 112 YouTube videos.

## Main Thesis

The core argument: "AI stops being a feature inside an interface and starts becoming the interface itself." Traditional graphical user interfaces are becoming obsolete as autonomous agents increasingly mediate user interactions.

## Ten Major Themes

### 1. Relentless Change
The defining characteristic of 2025 was accelerating transformation. UX professionals must develop adaptability as their primary competency.

### 2. The AI Career Pivot: Agency Over Titles
Professional advancement now requires "agency"--the ability to initiate action amid uncertainty. As execution costs approach zero through AI, human value concentrates in direction-setting.

### 3. User Research at Machine Speed
AI democratizes research through autonomous investigation. Human researchers increasingly focus on nuanced validation rather than initial data collection.

### 4. The Death of the User Interface (Generative UI)
The GUI paradigm, dominant since 1984, is effectively obsolete. Interaction shifts from command-based to intent-based. "The Best UI is No UI."

### 5. Time Is the Experience
Time represents the most neglected UX dimension. Two temporal modes: human think-time (to be protected) and machine think-time (requiring transparent communication).

### 6. Helping Users Say the Right Thing to AI
Prompting emerged as a literacy challenge. Effective scaffolding includes examples, labeled input slots, and feedback explaining model inference.

### 7. Creativity in the Age of AI
Creative work transforms from execution toward exploration. The "AI Sandwich" workflow: humans provide direction and curation while AI generates volume.

### 8. Visual Communication Goes Mainstream
AI image generation democratized visual communication beyond trained designers.

### 9. Transformative AI Economics
AI reshapes organizational structure. "Human-in-the-loop" review sometimes degrades outcomes rather than improving them.

### 10. Reaching Audiences in the Post-Website Era
Discovery shifts from PageRank to answer engines. Content must satisfy dual audiences: humans and AI systems.

## Six Major Mindset Shifts

1. **Competence Inversion**: From human verification of AI to non-interference
2. **Interface Extinction**: From static screens to generative interfaces
3. **Visual Singularity**: From text-based AI to visual communication
4. **Tempo Pivot**: From speed reduction to designing for latency
5. **Career Definition**: From skill acquisition to agency
6. **Latent-Space Navigation**: From articulation as bottleneck to exploration of possibility spaces
