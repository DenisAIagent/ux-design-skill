# The Big Tough UX-AI Quiz

**Date:** 2026-01-05

**Source:** [Original Article](https://jakobnielsenphd.substack.com/p/2025-quiz)

---

## Overview

Jakob Nielsen has created an extensive assessment containing "70 hard questions about the last year in UX and AI." The quiz invites readers to test their knowledge of developments spanning user experience design and artificial intelligence throughout 2025.

## Purpose and Instructions

Nielsen emphasizes that participants should "write down your answer" to each question before consulting the provided answers (in a separate article). This approach ensures genuine learning rather than relying on memory biases.

## Quiz Categories

The seventy questions cover diverse topics including:

**AI and UX Integration**
- Slow AI design patterns
- A/B testing with probabilistic AI outputs
- Human-AI collaboration dynamics
- Generative UI principles

**Professional Development**
- Career transitions in the AI era
- Emerging vs. obsolete skill sets
- Role evolution for designers and engineers

**Usability Fundamentals**
- UI metaphors and mental models
- Error message design
- Form optimization strategies
- Navigation patterns

**Emerging Concepts**
- Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)
- Prompt augmentation techniques
- Transformative AI economics
- AI scaling laws

**Practical Design**
- Email newsletter best practices
- Accessibility considerations
- Interface discoverability principles
- Testing methodologies
