2. **Research Reproducibility Crisis** - University of Copenhagen study found only 50% of CHI conference papers could be reproduced, aligning with broader academia trends.

3. **NotebookLM Video Performance** - Google's tool effectively transforms content into animated explainers with 76% audience retention past 30 seconds.

4. **Midjourney Version 8** - Long-awaited release disappoints creators. Slight text improvements pale against competitors; model has stagnated since V6 (December 2023).

5. **Micropayments for AI Agents** - New Machine Payments Protocol enables AI systems to access premium content through automated micropayments.

6. **Microsoft MAI-Image-2 & NVIDIA GTC** - Microsoft releases competitive image model; NVIDIA highlights GPU integration advances.
