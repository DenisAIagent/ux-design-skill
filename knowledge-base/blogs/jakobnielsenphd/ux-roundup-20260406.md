# UX Roundup: OpenAI Usability | Integrated Software | Seedance 2 vs. Kling 3 | AI Use

**Date:** 2026-04-06

**Source:** [Original Article](https://jakobnielsenphd.substack.com/p/ux-roundup-20260406)

## Topics

1. **OpenAI's Focus on Usability** - OpenAI announced plans to prioritize usability alongside capability, recognizing "the limiting factor shifts from intelligence to usability." Plans for an integrated AI superapp with persistent context.

2. **Integrated Software Principles** - Historical lessons from 1985 research: design for task integration, support discretionary users, accommodate satisficing behavior, minimize friction in data transfer.

3. **Video Model Comparison** - Seedance 2 superior to Kling 3 in cinematography, acting, and set design for Shakespeare scenes. Both struggle with archaic English pronunciation.

4. **Grok Imagine Updates** - Improved image/video model emphasizing higher quality as a less-censored alternative.

5. **AI Adoption Trends** - 78% of Americans use AI daily, 83% of employed workers utilize AI tools. Trust increased to 62%, though users remain hesitant about high-stakes decisions.
