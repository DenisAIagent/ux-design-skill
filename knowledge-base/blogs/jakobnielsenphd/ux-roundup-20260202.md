# UX Roundup: AI Judgment | Heuristic Evaluation | Consistent Brand Assets | New Music Model

**Date:** 2026-02-02

## Topics

1. **AI Judgment May Follow a Scaling Law** - Research demonstrates AI's judgment improves predictably with increased compute, suggesting judgment follows scaling patterns similar to other AI capabilities.

2. **Scaling AI's Judgment of Usability Heuristics** - Baymard Institute launched an AI service evaluating e-commerce sites against 154 usability guidelines at 95% accuracy--roughly 4x improvement over eight months.

3. **Generating Consistent Visual Design Assets with AI** - Luke Wroblewski created a Character Maker tool generating brand-consistent illustrations by analyzing requests, reformulating prompts, and validating outputs against design standards.

4. **New AI Music Model: Mureka O2** - New song-creation service offering richer musical sound, though Nielsen found Suno's mature editing capabilities still preferred.
