## Topics

1. **The AI is the Computer** - Perplexity founder proposes AI orchestration--not individual models--will become the primary "computer" solving user problems through multimodel systems.

2. **AI Disrupting Filmed Entertainment** - McKinsey projects roughly $60 billion in U.S. entertainment revenue disrupted within five years. Theatrical attendance drops 6% annually while social video grows 14%.

3. **2026 UX Trends Report** - World Usability Congress findings: 27% of companies fully realize Experience Design's potential; 67% report AI positively influences work.

4. **AI Can Conduct GOMS Analysis** - AI models like Gemini can now perform GOMS evaluations affordably, making this cognitive science method practical for UX professionals.

5. **Nano Banana 2 & Lyria 3** - Google updates: improved image generation with 25% cost reduction; music generation limited to 30-second clips.
