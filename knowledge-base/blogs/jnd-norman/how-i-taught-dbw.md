# How I Taught DBW (Design for a Better World)

**Date:** June 4, 2025
**Author:** Don Norman
**Source:** https://jnd.org/how-i-taught-dbw/

## Teaching Methodology

Unconventional structure: students divided into six groups per book section, each leading weekly discussions.

Brief quizzes via Google Forms — 4 questions, 5 minutes. "Even wrong answers could indicate the student had read the material and was trying to make sense of it."

This "turned out to be a wonderful way of developing rich, detailed discussion."

## Course Outcomes

- Winter 2021: Student presentations compiling supplementary resources
- Spring 2022: Website prototype in Figma (developed by UX Consultancy Ltd.)

## The Four Original Maxims

Quality-of-life measurement, sustainability, behavioral change, and community-centered collaboration.
